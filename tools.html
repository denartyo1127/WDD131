Ways to Cope

  • Try deep breathing exercises (e.g., inhale for 4, exhale for 4).
  • Use grounding techniques like 5-4-3-2-1.
  • Explore mental health apps like Calm or Headspace.
  • Talk to a trusted friend or counselor.
